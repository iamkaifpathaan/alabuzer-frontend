Refund & Cancellation Policy

Order Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placing the order. After dispatch, cancellation is not possible.

Returns

Due to the nature of fragrance products, returns are only accepted if the product is damaged or defective upon delivery.

Refund Process

Once the returned item is received and inspected, the refund will be processed within 5-7 business days.

Non-Refundable Items

Opened or used products are not eligible for refunds due to hygiene and safety reasons.
